About BluePeak Realty

Kenya's trusted real estate partner since 2010

Founded in 2010 by a team of passionate real estate professionals, BluePeak Realty has grown to become one of Kenya's most respected property agencies. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make real estate transactions transparent, efficient, and stress-free for all parties involved.

Over the past 14 years, we've helped over 5,000 clients navigate the Kenyan property market, from first-time homebuyers to large-scale investors. Our team of 85+ experts brings together diverse skills in property valuation, legal services, market analysis, and customer relations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I view a property?

We can arrange viewings within 24-48 hours for most properties. Virtual tours are available immediately for many listings.

What are your fees for buyers?

For property buyers, our services are typically free. We're compensated by the seller. There are no hidden fees.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with expatriates and international investors, including handling remote transactions.

How do you verify property titles?

We conduct thorough due diligence at the Ministry of Lands and work with licensed advocates to verify all documentation.
